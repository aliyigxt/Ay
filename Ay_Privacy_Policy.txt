Privacy Policy for Ay

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ay (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information. Ay is designed as a personal workspace, conversation organizer, content library, planning tool, camera/document utility, and Apple Intelligence-supported assistant experience with a local-first approach.

We do not sell your data. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, third-party crash analytics, data brokers, or cross-app tracking.

Most App data is stored locally on your device. The App does not operate a custom backend server and does not provide a server-based account system. Some data may be processed by Apple-managed services only when you choose to use features that depend on iOS, Apple Intelligence, StoreKit, Calendar, Reminders, Maps, Photos, Speech, Spotlight, Siri, Shortcuts, App Store, iCloud device backup, or other Apple system services.
