Quick Help

Apple Intelligence features are not available

Apple Intelligence availability depends on Apple-supported devices, languages, regions, iOS settings, and system requirements.

Notifications are not appearing

Check iOS notification permissions, Focus modes, scheduled summaries, low power mode, and App notification settings.

Calendar or Reminders sync is not working

Make sure Calendar and Reminders permissions are enabled for Ay, then check your Apple Calendar, Reminders, iCloud, and account sync settings.

Camera, Photos, Files, or Microphone access is blocked

Open iOS Settings, find Ay, and review the related permissions. The App can only access features you allow.

Before contacting support

Include your device model, iOS version, App version, a short description of the issue, and the steps that caused it.
